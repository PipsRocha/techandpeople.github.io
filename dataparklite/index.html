DataparkLite
A report generator for accerolemeter data
With this tool you will be able to get a personalized report based on the metrics we are using (or adapted with your own). Now you can give a meaning to the raw data you collect.

Metrics Examples
Wear Time

Non-wear time periods are removed from subsequent analysis. Wear time of less than 75% of a day is a criterion for exclusion of that complete day from further analysis.

For more details see   van Hees et al. 2011
Immobility Measures
Immobility measures are captured resorting to the Estimation of Stationary Sleep segments algorithm. From this segmentation, several measures can be derived:
  • Night Time Immobility Duration (NTID)
  • Night Time Mobility Periods and Duration (NTMP and NTMD)
  • Diurnal Immobility Periods and Duration (DIP and DID)
Additionally, other variables can be derived from the activity signal:
  • Intradaily variability (IV)
  • Interdaily stability (IS)
  • Relative amplitude (RA)

For more details see:

  • Jones, Hare, and Evershed 2005
  • Gonçalves et al. 2014
  • Borazio et al. 2014
Activity Measures
Activity measures reflect the amount and robustness of physical activity performed by the subjects. We derive the following measures :
  • Daily Physical Activity (DPA)
  • Nightly Physical Activity (NPA)
  • Daily Jerk (DJ)
For more details see   O’Brien et al. 2017
